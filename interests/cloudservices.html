Cloud Services

Iaas

Iaas is one of the four servide models in cloud computing the others being Paas, Saas and Caas.

Iaas standing for infrastructure as a service can be used in multiple ways such as testing and developing, hosting websites, web applications and storage and backup.

The provider hosts a cloud service with infrastructure such as servers, networking hardware and storage.

Iaas involves building IT platforms through the cloud and has numerous benefits such as scalability, secure data, reduced costs and increased time.

Paas

Paas stands for platform as a service and involves a provider administrating both hardware and software tools to a user.

Paas is most commonly used for building applications and servers.

Paas ensures the client doesn't have to invest heavily in their own infrastructure as well as being adaptive, flexible and secure.

Saas

Saas standing for software as a service involves a provider hosting applications for a client over the internet.

Saas may be used commonly for planning, communications and tracking sales.

Advantages of Saas include its scalability, accessability and automatic updates.

Caas

Caas stands for communications as a service and involves providers supplying software for communications.

Caas may include voice and video calls, conferencing and broadcasting.

Caas provides scalability, global accessability and business continuity in the event of an unexpected occurence.
